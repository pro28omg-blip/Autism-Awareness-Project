Awareness

Autism awareness focuses on understanding how individuals experience the world differently. It promotes acceptance, inclusion, and respect for neurodiversity.

In an IGCSE Global Perspectives context, awareness means recognizing challenges faced by autistic individuals and understanding their strengths rather than viewing autism as a limitation.

About

Autism is a spectrum condition affecting communication, behavior, and social interaction. Each individual has unique strengths and challenges.

This project explores autism awareness globally and examines how different societies respond to it.

Global Issue

Autism is a global issue because support systems differ greatly between countries. Some regions provide strong support, while others lack awareness and resources.
