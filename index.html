Autism Awareness

Understanding perspectives, challenges, and inclusion

📌 About This Project

This Global Perspectives project explores autism awareness, focusing on global understanding, challenges, and solutions for inclusion.

🌍 Global Issue

Autism is a neurodevelopmental condition that affects communication and behavior. Awareness varies across countries.

👀 Different Perspectives

Some countries have strong support systems, while others still face stigma and lack of awareness.

💡 Solutions

Education, inclusion, early diagnosis, and awareness campaigns can improve lives significantly.

📊 Key Message

Every individual with autism experiences the world differently. Inclusion and understanding matter.
