Autism Awareness

Understanding perspectives, challenges, and inclusion

🌍 Global Issue

Autism is a developmental condition affecting millions of people worldwide. Awareness and understanding vary across countries and cultures.

👀 Different Perspectives

Some societies provide strong support systems, while others still lack awareness, education, and acceptance.

💡 Solutions

Education, inclusion policies, early diagnosis, and awareness campaigns can improve quality of life and social acceptance.
