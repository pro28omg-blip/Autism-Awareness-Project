Autism Awareness

Understanding perspectives, challenges, and inclusion

📌 About This Project

This Global Perspectives project explores autism awareness, focusing on global understanding, challenges, and solutions for inclusion.

🌍 Global Issue

Autism is a neurodevelopmental condition that affects communication and behavior. Awareness and diagnosis vary across countries due to differences in education and healthcare systems.

👀 Different Perspectives

Some societies are highly supportive with strong inclusion systems, while others still face stigma and limited understanding of autism.

💡 Solutions

Better education, awareness campaigns, inclusive schools, and early diagnosis can greatly improve support for individuals with autism.

📊 Key Message

Every individual with autism experiences the world differently. Inclusion and understanding are essential for a fair society.
